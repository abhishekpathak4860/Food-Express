Dahi Bade

Dahi Vada or Dahi Bhalla is an immensely popular Indian chaat dish. This protein packed, gluten-free recipe is made from lentil fritters dunked in whipped creamy yogurt drizzled with sweet and spicy chutney and spices. Its served as a snack, appetizer or side with main umeal and is absolutely melt in mouth delicious!!
